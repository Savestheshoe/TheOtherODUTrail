The ODU Trail

Fiscal Year 2026-2027
You have 1 year to get your program started, running efficiently, and serving the community while staying within budget! Can you survive, THE ODU TRAIL?

HOW TO PLAY

YOUR GOALS

  • Achieve high Community Buy-In (Overall and across all 4 Area Types).
  • Maintain high Strategic Alignment and Program Effectiveness.
  • End the fiscal year with as much surplus budget as possible.
  • Ensure equitable service by visiting all Area Types (Urban, Suburban, Rural, Frontier).
  • Pass your specific Program Objective to ensure renewal!

ACTIONS & UI

Use the bottom control panel to take turns. Each action advances time by 2 weeks (1 round).

1. DEPLOY VAN (Field Work)

Select a destination city and an activity, then click Deploy. Costs Fuel + Activity Fee.

  • Town Hall (buy-in): Increases Buy-In via narrative choices. Costs $1,500 + Fuel.
  • Direct Service (effectiveness): Action minigame for Effectiveness. Grants base +5 Local Buy-In. Costs $1,000 + Fuel.
  • Meet with CBOs (alignment): Action minigame for Alignment. Grants base +5 Local Buy-In. Costs $400 + Fuel.

2. ENGAGE ODU (Administration)

Travel to the PSB to boost your program. Costs $3,000 flat.

  • Analyze Data (EPIs): Identifies targeted city/action combos that yield a +10 Buy-In bonus for the next 2 rounds.
  • PM / QI: Permanently reduces overhead burn rate by 20% AND adds +1 to ALL minigame points and Town Hall rewards!
  • Strategic Alignment: Instantly boosts Alignment by 30% AND permanently raises the max score cap of all minigames by +2!

PERFORMANCE CHECKS

  • Bi-Monthly Check (Every 4 rounds/8 weeks): If Buy-In is < 60, you lose Effectiveness. If Alignment is High (60%+ or 85%+), you earn $5,000 or $10,000 in bonus funding!
  • Annual Evaluation (Round 24): You must pass 3 of 5 core criteria PLUS your program's Special Objective to win.
